Automation, PLC, electrical and marine engineering services.

About

Mictech Ab is a small engineering consultancy providing technical design, implementation and commissioning services within automation and electrical systems. The focus is on robust, well-documented solutions for marine and industrial applications.

Services

Automation & PLC

  • PLC programming and commissioning
  • System architecture and I/O design
  • Diagnostics, alarms and monitoring
  • Legacy system support

Electrical Engineering

  • Electrical system design
  • Power distribution and protection
  • Control panels and schematics
  • Documentation and FAT/SAT support

Marine & Industrial

  • Marine automation systems
  • Monitoring and safety systems
  • Integration of sensors and field devices
  • Commissioning and troubleshooting

Working Method

Emphasis is placed on clarity, maintainability and reliability. Solutions are developed with long-term operation, serviceability and documentation in mind. Close cooperation with yards, suppliers and end customers is a key part of each project.
